Technical Skills

Ceramics
loading/unloading kilns, kiln maintenance and repair, firing in oxidation and reduction, wheel maintenance and repair, clay recycling, glaze making, glaze recipe authoring, building cone packs. Also proficient in throwing, casting, and hand building techniques.
Woodworking
proficient with band saw, miter saw, table saw, belt and drum sander, panel saw, hand and power tools, applying wood finishes. Capable of basic maintenance.
Textiles
hand and machine sewing, machine servicing, weaving, crochet
Printmaking
ferric chloride/copper etching, woodcut, linoleum, stone lithography, silkscreen. Press upkeep and small repairs, brayer and roller maintenance.
Metal
metal fabrication, MIG welding, CNC plasma, typical plasma cutting, replacing shielding gas, replacing consumables, basic lab maintenance.
Plaster
sculptural techniques such as life casting and hand building, ceramics techniques
Photography
35mm SLR film photography, cyanotype on paper and fabric
Gallery Prep
resurfacing & painting walls, installing work at 57/60 center, installing in salon style, safely moving large mobile walls
Computer
